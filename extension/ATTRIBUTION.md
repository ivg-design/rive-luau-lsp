## Luau

[Luau](https://github.com/luau-lang/luau) is a fast, small, safe, gradually typed embeddable scripting language derived from Lua, created by Roblox Corporation.

- **License:** MIT
- **Repository:** https://github.com/luau-lang/luau

## Rive Type Definitions

The Rive Luau type definitions (`rive-globals.d.luau`) are custom definitions for the Rive scripting API, based on the [LERP course documentation](https://rive.app/community/doc/scripting-overview/docbFP0MSVxL) and [Rive's official documentation](https://rive.app/docs).
